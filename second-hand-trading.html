How It Works

Four simple steps from listing your item to settlement — shown as a demo workflow

📦
Step 1

List & Mint

Connect your wallet, describe your item, and set a price. AI suggests a sample market range. The app creates an NFT-style ownership record in the demo listing flow.

🛒
Step 2

Browse & Buy

Buyers browse listings with demo verification badges. Purchase can be simulated with eHKD, USDC, eCNY, or FPS options. Funds and ownership transfer are represented in a staged UI flow.

🚚
Step 3

Ship & Scan

Seller ships the item with a QR code. Delivery confirmation is represented as a conceptual off-chain event in the UI to trigger the next demo step.

✅
Step 4

Confirm & Settle

Buyer confirms receipt (or auto-confirms after 7 days). The UI demonstrates a sample settlement split: 97% seller payout, 1.5% platform fee, and 1.5% dispute reserve.

Settlement Flow (Conceptual)

This section illustrates a possible contract-based workflow for demo and classroom discussion

🏷️

Seller Lists Item — NFT-Style Record Created

Seller connects wallet and submits listing. Identity, AML, and NFT minting are represented as conceptual checks in this prototype rather than live external integrations.

🛒

Buyer Purchases — Simulated Escrow Allocation

Buyer payment options and compliance checks are shown as product concepts. The UI models a settlement split of 97% seller, 1.5% platform fee, and 1.5% dispute reserve.

📦

Delivery Confirmed — QR Scan Demo Event

Courier scan and oracle confirmation are presented as conceptual integration points. This page simulates delivery confirmation to move the workflow forward.

✅

Settlement or Dispute Handling (Conceptual)

After buyer confirmation, the demo applies the same sample split and updates ownership status in UI. Dispute handling is shown as a governance concept, not an active on-chain vote.

Trust & Protection

Trovex shows multiple trust layers as a product concept for safer peer-to-peer transactions

🛡️

Compliance Screening (Concept)

Buyer and seller checks are shown as conceptual workflow steps. No live Chainalysis or RegTech API is integrated in this page.

🪪

SSI / ZKP Identity

Self-Sovereign Identity and Zero-Knowledge Proof let users prove they are real, KYC-compliant HK residents without revealing any personal data to counterparties.

🤖

AI Fraud Detection

AI fraud checks are represented as a concept. Listing analysis in this demo is illustrative and does not execute production anti-fraud models.

🔗

Delivery Oracle (Concept)

Delivery confirmation is modeled as an oracle-style integration point. Settlement shown on this page is simulated.

⭐

Reputation Layer

Reputation updates are shown in the interface as an example trust mechanism for marketplace design discussions.

⚖️

Governance Dispute Model

Token-holder voting is described as a possible governance approach. It is not wired to a live DAO contract in this demo.
